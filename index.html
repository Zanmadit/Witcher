Welcome to the world of The Witcher

The player is the protagonist of Sapkowski's literary world - the witcher Geralt of Rivia. The action of the game unfolds after the events of the witcher saga - barely surviving,
Geralt falls into amnesia and is forced to re-learn his craft. The player recovers the lost experience of the protagonist, fighting human opponents and monsters.
At the same time, he is faced with a moral choice, on which the further fate of the game world depends.
The game faithfully reflects the dark atmosphere of the universe of novels, but its plot was not officially supported by Sapkowski.
